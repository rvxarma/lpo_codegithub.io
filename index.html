/ Why Outsource?

/When it comes to dispute resolution, pools of data have to be checked for discovery / disclosure, production or fact-gathering.

Accordingly, elements of document review, contract scanning and litigation consulting are necessary for litigation, arbitration as well as investigations. Legal process goes much further than simple relevance and due diligence. It typically requires complex, subjective analysis and allocation of documents to issues in accordance with guidance and criteria provided by the client.

Partner with Glocal LPO for cost-effective global managed services and technology. We take on routine legal and business matters supported by our experienced lawyers for legal oversight and escalation.
