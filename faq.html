What can we do for you?

Outsourcing your legal woes helps you to concentrate on your primary business while also providing your organization access to higher-level knowledge than you could normally afford. It entails partnering with qualified attorneys who are suitably skilled to handle legal business operations, as well as the ability to retain them for a lengthy period of time. Having a trusted legal outsourcing partner might also help you save money on overhead expenses.

Glocal LPO is a team of qualified Indian attorneys, all of whom have completed integrated BA LLB (Hons) and BA LLM (Hons) from top Indian Universities and National Law Schools.
Our lawyers are backed by years of professional experience and we also arrange regular training programs to get them acquainted with the Common Law System in the UK, US, and Canada.
Our team is capable of handling projects that require special skills like research and analysis as they hold master's degrees in engineering, finance, business administration, statistics, research, communication and English literature.

We have a well-defined model of operation which ensures that our clients have complete control over their projects. A Team Lead contacts the customer directly as soon as the project is accepted and keeps in touch with them throughout the project's completion.
You can always connect with us using email or telephone for any assistance.

Every client signs a Service Level Agreement (SLA) with us to develop a clear understanding of quality, time, deliverables, priorities, responsibilities, performance, billing and more.
